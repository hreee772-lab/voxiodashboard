Widget Customization
Embed and customize your Voicera widget with advanced CSS and event hooks.
STEP 1
Add the Script Tag
Paste this inside the <head> or before the closing </body> tag of your website.
<script src="https://voicera-landing.teamvoicera7.workers.dev/src/widget.js"></script>
STEP 2
Initialize the Widget
Add this snippet right after the script tag. Your unique clientId is already filled in.
<script>
  new VoiceraWidget({
    clientId: 'YOUR_CLIENT_ID',
    backendUrl: 'https://voicera-dashboard-production.up.railway.app'
  });
</script>
STEP 3
Customize via Bot Config
Go to Bot Config in the sidebar to set your bot's name, greeting message, escalation keywords, voice model, and response style. All changes reflect instantly on your widget.
STEP 4
Upload Your Knowledge Base
Upload PDFs, DOCX, or TXT files in the Knowledge Base section. The AI will use these to answer your customers' questions accurately.
STEP 5
Test Your Widget
Once embedded, open your website and click the Voicera widget bubble. Try chatting, test a voice call, and trigger an escalation keyword to see the booking flow in action.
