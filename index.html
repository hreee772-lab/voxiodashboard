How long does it take to set up Voxio?
+
Under 5 minutes. Copy one line of code, paste it into your website, and Voxio is live. No technical knowledge needed.

+
No. If you can paste text into your website, you can install Voxio. It works on any website or platform — no engineering team required.

+
Voxio automatically raises a ticket to the right team, schedules a callback if needed, and follows up with your customer until the issue is completely closed. Nothing is left open.

+
Voxio is built for early stage startups and growing SMBs — especially in SaaS, e-commerce, and service businesses that need serious customer support without the enterprise price tag.
Is my customer data secure?

Yes. All conversations are encrypted end to end. Your data is stored securely and never shared with third parties.
